Single Sign-On (SSO) Setup

Acme Cloud supports SAML 2.0 and OIDC for SSO. SSO is included on the Business
plan ($49/user/month) and the Enterprise plan. The Starter plan does not include SSO.

To configure SAML SSO:
1. In your IdP (Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace), create a new SAML application.
2. Set the ACS URL to https://app.acme.cloud/sso/saml/acs.
3. Set the Entity ID to acme-cloud.
4. Upload the IdP metadata XML in Settings > Security > SSO.
5. Map the email attribute to NameID.

SCIM provisioning is available on Enterprise only and is configured separately
under Settings > Security > SCIM.

Support response time for SSO issues is 4 business hours on Business, 1 hour on Enterprise.
