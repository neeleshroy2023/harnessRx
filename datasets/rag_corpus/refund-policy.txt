Acme Cloud Refund Policy

Customers may request a full refund within 14 days of the initial purchase for
any reason. After 14 days, refunds are prorated based on unused service time
for annual plans only; monthly plans are non-refundable after the 14-day window.

Refunds for enterprise contracts (>$10,000 annual value) require written
approval from a Customer Success Manager and are processed within 30 business days.

Refunds are returned to the original payment method. Bank wire refunds are not
supported for transactions originally paid via credit card.
