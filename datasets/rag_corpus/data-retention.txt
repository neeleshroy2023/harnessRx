Data Retention and Deletion

Customer data is retained for the duration of the active subscription plus a
30-day grace period after cancellation. After the grace period, all customer
data is permanently deleted from primary storage within 7 days and from backups
within 90 days.

Audit logs are retained for 12 months on the Business plan and 24 months on the
Enterprise plan. Audit logs are immutable and cannot be deleted by customers.

Customers may request immediate data deletion (right-to-be-forgotten) by emailing
privacy@acme.cloud. Such requests are completed within 30 days of receipt and a
written confirmation is sent.
